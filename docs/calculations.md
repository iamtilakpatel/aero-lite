# Performance Calculations

These calculations were done to check whether the system could realistically support a small crew and to validate the design choices made earlier.

---

## 1. Crew CO₂ Production

NASA Standard Metabolic Rate: 1 astronaut produces ~1.0 kg CO₂ per day under nominal activity.

4-Person Crew:
4 crew × 1.0 kg/day = 4.0 kg CO₂/day

Hourly Production Rate:
4.0 kg/day ÷ 24 hr = 0.167 kg/hr = 167 g/hr

AERO-LITE Target: ~150 g/hr capture rate provides ~90% of crew CO₂ output, with margin for metabolic variation.
